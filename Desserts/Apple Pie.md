Apple Pie

Crust

•	1 stick butter
•	1.5 - 2 cups sugar
•	1 large egg
•	1 cup self rising flour
•	1 cup all purpose flour

1.	soften butter,
2.	combine/mix/cream butter, 1 cup sugar/egg put in bowl
3.	sift self rising and all purpose flour together
4.	Mix Flour & Creamed sugar together a little at a time
5.	Grease & Flour pie Plate
o	Dump flour into pie plate, spread out evenly and up the sides
6.	Add apples
7.	Coat top with remaining crust
8.	Cook @ 350 for 45 minutes
o	turn off oven but leave pie in for another 45 minutes to finish cooking. (Prevents bottom crust from burning).


Filling

•	2 ½ lb granny smith apple, cored, sliced, peeled
•	¾ cup sugar
•	2 tablespoons flour
•	½ teaspoon salt
•	1 teaspoon cinnamon
•	¼ teaspoon nutmeg
•	½ lemon
•	1 egg, beaten
•	1 tablespoon sugar

1.	Peel the apples, then core and slice.
2.	In a bowl, add the sliced apples, sugar, flour, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and juice from the lemon.
3.	Mix until combined and all apples are coated. Refrigerate.
